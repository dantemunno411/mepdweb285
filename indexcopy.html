Crissel Takes Over New York

This short documentary will follow a woman named Crissel Morales, a true entrepreneur who paved her own way in the hair styling industry. This short documentary will start from her humble beginnings, from where she was born and raised, to earning her hair styling licence, working and styling hair in the back of nail salons, opening her own shop and hiring apprentices and interns, how Covid-19 impact her business, how she was able to adapt and where she is going with "Crissel New York" in the future.
